Land coastline networks (CLNs)

Terrestrial coastline networks (CLNs) is one of structures of marine technology development. A terrestrial coastline network is composed of infrastructures like BSs installed near or along coastlines or islands. CLNs connect the offshore maritime network and land network communications, and provide wireless services for water users. I implemented a simulation test with the Exata simulation package.
Firstly, load one existed sea path and Donghai Bridge according to Shanghai offshore fields. Secondly, use WiMAX and LTE technology to build the scenario respectively and calculate and analyze the end-to-end delay, jitter and throughput of different scenarios. Finally, summarize the better plan to build the CLNs.
